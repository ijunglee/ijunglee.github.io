About Me

I-Jung Lee received the Ph.D. degree in College of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from National Taipei University, New Taipei City, Taiwan, in 2022. She is also an honorary associate in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Wisconsin, Madison, WI, USA. Her research interests are in the areas of embedded systems and wearable devices applied to biomedical engineering.
